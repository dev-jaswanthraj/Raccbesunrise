About Program

"Each new day is a opportunity to improve yourself."

One fit is a Sunriser's Fitness challenge. The participant should workout daily and update how much calories they burned per day in our whatsapp group we will track the participants daily. At the end of the month the participant who burned the high amount calories will be rewarded.


Participants should continuously participate in this 30 Day Fitness Challenge mandatory on a streak.


Your performance will be analysed through Starva Application (Available for both iOS and Android).


You should perform Running, Walking, Riding by activating your GPS in the App.


At the end of the day you should Screenshot your activity performed.
